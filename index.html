About Todd Lemoine

I'm a conscientious developer who enjoys working on projects that help people. I live in Portland with my wife and our two sons, and work as a senior software engineer at Jama Software, helping shepherd an app that helps organizations like SpaceX, NASA, Boeing and Caterpillar build their next-generation products. I'm focused on making the app more inclusive by improving its accessibility.

I work mostly in Javascript, node, React, and the DOM, and I generally love it. I believe the most important attributes of an application are how empowered it makes users feel and how well it executes on that promise. These days, I'm interested in accessibility, RxJs, and functional programming.

You can see examples of my code in this ToDo application, or in this War card game. Both were written as simple exercises, but should give you a feel for how I write and think. Source code is available here and here.

I also wrote A Strange Game, a book about my own take on modern life and how to navigate it. You can buy it from LeanPub or Amazon.
